Cheeseburger

Description

This is how to create a delicious cheeseburger. I love cheeseburgers so be sure to follow this word for word. Once you first take a bite you will know this is the best recipe ever.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Bread or buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Cheese
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Form the beef into patties
  2. Cook the patties on the oven
  3. Build the burger with the above ingredients
