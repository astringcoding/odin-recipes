Wings

Description

This is how to create delicious wings. I love wings so be sure to follow this word for word. Once you first take a bite you will know this is the best recipe ever.

Ingredients

  • Hot sauce
  • Chicken
  • Breading
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Combine the kosher salt with the hot sauce
  2. Bread the chicken
  3. Throw the chicken into the frier
  4. Take the chicken out and rub in the hot sauce mix
  5. Enjoy
