Pizza

Description

This is how to create a delicious cheese pizza. I love pizza so be sure to follow this word for word. Once you first take a bite you will know this is the best recipe ever.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Form the dough
  2. Heat up the sauce and spread on the dough
  3. Sprinkle the cheese on the sauce
  4. Top with pepperoni
  5. Cook the pizza in the oven
